Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that has captured the hearts and taste buds of people around the world.

This beloved comfort food is a hearty and flavorful meal that is perfect for sharing family and friends.

Lasagna Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Spaghetti Sauce
  • Cheeses
    • Cottage Cheese
    • Mozzarella
    • Parmesan
  • Eggs
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Water

Steps to make Lasagna

  1. Cook and drain the ground beef, then stir in the spaghetti sauce and simmer.
  2. Combine the cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella, eggs, half of the Parmesan, and seasonings.
  3. Assemble the lasagna according to the detailed recipe.
  4. Bake, covered, for 45 minutes.
  5. Uncover and continue baking for 10 minutes.
